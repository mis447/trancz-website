Know About Us

Trancz Fashion, based in Mumbai, India, is a leading garment manufacturing company specializing in premium apparel production, fabric sourcing, and private label solutions. Founded by Rohit Jain, Trancz Fashion is a GST-registered Indian manufacturer delivering high-quality, design-driven clothing for domestic brands and businesses. We specialize in end-to-end garment development — from concept and pattern design to stitching, finishing, and packaging — offering complete FOB and DDP solutions for fashion, lifestyle, and corporate clients.

Trancz's Vision and Mission

Trancz Fashion is focused on providing our customers with quality, fashion garments through hassle free process at best possible Turn around time. Our systems, processes, management team, expertise makes us ONE-STOP Solutions for brands meeting their requierments right from sourcing, pattern making , sampling and production to finalizing delivery partners.

At Trancz Fashion, we understand that brands need a manufacturing partner who is reliable, efficient, and aligned with their creative vision. Delays, miscommunication, or inconsistent quality can slow down a brand’s growth and impact its reputation. That’s why we have built robust management structures, streamlined systems, and a culture of on-time delivery — ensuring every project runs smoothly from concept to final product.

Our Facility

Over the years, Trancz Fashion has collaborated with a wide range of streetwear and fast-fashion brandss, providing end-to-end services including fabric and textile sourcing, design development, size chart finalization, and pattern making.

We operate from a 4,500 sq. ft. office space, equipped with 35 modern machines and a dedicated team of 32 skilled professionals, organized into Onboarding, Sampling, and Production departments to ensure seamless workflow.

Our Production Team, comprising experienced karigars and bulk-cutting masters, delivers fast, efficient, and large-scale manufacturing without compromising on quality.
